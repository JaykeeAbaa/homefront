Frequently asked questions

Clear, straightforward answers—no jargon.

Will a claim make my premiums go up?

It depends on your policy and the cause of damage. We’ll help you understand options and talk with your adjuster so you can make an informed decision.

How long does installation take?

Most projects are completed in 1–2 days, weather permitting. We protect landscaping and ensure a thorough magnet sweep for nails.

Do you offer financing?

Yes. We can walk you through options that fit your budget so you can move forward with confidence.

What if I’m not sure I have damage?

That’s exactly why we start with a free inspection. We’ll show photo evidence and give a clear recommendation—no pressure.
